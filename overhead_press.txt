Steps for Overhead Press:
With your hands shoulder-width apart, squat and grip the weight bar.
Lift the bar slowly to your chest or shoulders.
Take a deep breath and lift it over your head until your arms are fully stretched. Keep your elbows locked.
Slowly lower the bar to the shoulder level.
Do 3 reps or as per your comfort level. You can do this exercise with dumbbells as well.
Target Muscles: Deltoids, Triceps, and Upper back muscles.
Precautions: Do not perform this exercise if you have a neck or shoulder injury.
